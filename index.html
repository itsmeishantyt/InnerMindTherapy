About InnerMindTherapy

InnerMindTherapy offers trauma-informed, evidence-based therapy tailored to young adults and professionals. We prioritise clear communication, safety, and a calm environment where you can process experiences and build skills.

What we offer

  • 1:1 trauma-focused therapy (online & in-person where available)
  • Short-term stabilisation plans after difficult events
  • Tools for anxiety, sleep issues and flashbacks
  • Clinical letters and onward referral where appropriate

Who it's for

  • Students balancing study and wellbeing
  • Professionals dealing with burnout or crisis
  • People recovering from sudden or long-term trauma
  • Anyone who wants confidential, non-judgemental support

Meet the team

Our therapists are trained in trauma-informed approaches (EMDR, CBT and integrative counselling). We match you with the right therapist based on your needs and availability.
